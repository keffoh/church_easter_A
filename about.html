About Our Church

Our History

The Lord's Awareness Chapel International was founded in 1995 by Pastor John Smith with a vision to create a place of worship where people could experience the tangible presence of God.

Starting with just a handful of members meeting in a small rented space, the church has grown to become a vibrant community of believers with multiple campuses across the region.

Our Beliefs

The Bible

We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible, authoritative Word of God.

The Trinity

We believe in one God eternally existing in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

Salvation

We believe salvation is received through repentance toward God and faith in Jesus Christ.

The Church

We believe the Church is the body of Christ with a divine mission to preach the gospel to all nations.
