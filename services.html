Our Services

Regular Services

Sunday Morning Worship

8:00 AM & 10:30 AM

Our Sunday services are the highlight of the week, featuring vibrant worship, prayer, and biblical teaching. Come experience the presence of God with us!

Wednesday Bible Study

6:00 PM

Midweek Bible study provides deeper understanding of God's Word and its application to daily life. Bring your questions and join the discussion!

Tuesday Prayer Meeting

6:00 PM

Prayer is the foundation of our church. Join us as we intercede for our church, community, nation, and personal needs.

Friday Youth Service

5:00 PM

A dynamic service designed for youth with relevant teaching, worship, and fellowship in a Christ-centered environment.

Special Services

Communion Service

First Sunday of each month during morning worship

We celebrate the Lord's Supper together as we remember Christ's sacrifice and proclaim His death until He comes again.

Baptism Service

Quarterly (Next: June 25, 2023)

Public declaration of faith through water baptism. If you're interested in being baptized, please contact the church office.

Annual Revival

October 15-18, 2023

A week of special services with guest speakers, focused on spiritual renewal and awakening in our church and community.
